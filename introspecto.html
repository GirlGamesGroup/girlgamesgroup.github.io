Introspecto

Introspecto is a text based adventure game with management mechanics where the player is a writer who has to balance social and private live in order to get to the end of the game. The ending of the game are defined by the social decisions of the player.

  • Platform: PC / mobile
  • Genre: Adventure, management
  • Engine: Twine
  • Mode: Singleplayer
  • Programmer: Clara Cerqueira, Isabella Rodolfo
  • Game Designer: Isabella Rodolfo
